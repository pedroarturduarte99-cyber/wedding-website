Our Story

Two Cities.
One Great Love.

🌊 Rio de Janeiro 🍇 Douro Valley 🏛 Lisbon 🌷 Amsterdam
Your photo here Replace in Canva

She grew up with the warmth of Rio in her bones — samba, sun, and the kind of laughter that fills an entire room. He grew up among the ancient quintas of the Douro Valley, where time moves slowly, vines grow patiently, and good wine is never rushed. Two people who could not have been more different, on two sides of an ocean, entirely unaware that Lisbon was already waiting for them.

Lisbon, that relentlessly beautiful city, has always had a talent for making strangers feel like they've come home. It did exactly that for both of them — and, one ordinary evening that became anything but ordinary, it introduced them to each other. They talked until the trams stopped running. They have not stopped talking since.

Today, they call Amsterdam home — a city of canals, bicycles, and one extremely opinionated Romanian rescue dog. And on the 10th of September 2027, they will say their vows where the story has always belonged: under the Portuguese sky, surrounded by the people they love most, with very good wine and absolutely no plan to stop dancing until dawn.

· · · Getting Here & Staying Over
✈️ Getting There

The nearest airport is Francisco Sá Carneiro (Porto), approximately 1h15 from the venue. We recommend flying into Porto and renting a car, or joining a shuttle. A transfer from Porto city centre will be available — details to follow.

🏡 Where to Stay

Quinta do Paço has rooms available for guests at a special rate: Single €90 · Double €110. For more options, Vila Real town centre is a 10-minute drive. Book early — September in the Douro goes fast.

Where the Magic Happens

Quinta do Paço

Nestled in the hills of Vila Real, Quinta do Paço is a centuries-old manor surrounded by vineyards, terraced gardens, and sweeping views of the Douro Valley. It is exactly the kind of place where memories are made and toasts are repeated.

Dress Code

Garden Party Elegance

"Think: the best version of yourself enjoying a warm September evening in a Portuguese quinta. Soft, earthy tones and florals are warmly encouraged. And please — white is taken."

We love to see ✨
  • 🌿 Linen suits & relaxed blazers
  • 👗 Flowy midi & maxi dresses
  • 🌸 Floral prints & soft pastels
  • 🎨 Earthy tones, sage, terracotta, dusty blue
  • 👡 Wedge heels or block heels (cobblestones!)
  • 🌙 Cardigan for after midnight — Douro nights are dreamy
Please avoid 🙏
  • 🤍 White, ivory, or cream
  • 🎩 Black tie / ultra-formal
  • 👠 Stilettos (outdoor cobblestone venue)
  • 👖 Denim (save it for Sunday brunch)
  • 🏖 Very casual or beachwear
  • 💼 Anything that looks like a Monday
